# "Through the Keyhole" — Hook Concept

## Overview
5-second branded TikTok hook for Helmusweg 12, Venlo. Homi brand (neon green #00FF00 + black #000000).
Camera flies through a keyhole in the front door, revealing the stunning interior. The brand is woven into the visual device — the keyhole outline, the green accents — not slapped on top.

## The Story
Ordinary Dutch suburban house on the outside. Extraordinary luxury interior on the inside. The keyhole is the device that bridges these two worlds — you're peeking into something you weren't supposed to see.

## Beat Sheet

### 0.0-0.5s — THE STOP
- Black screen with a keyhole shape at center, glowing warm golden light from inside
- Homi logo small in corner — neon green on black
- First frame must be unlike anything else in a TikTok feed: dark, mysterious, the keyhole is the only light
- **Purpose:** Pattern interrupt. Stop the scroll.

### 0.5-2.0s — THE PEEK
- Camera pushes into the keyhole
- Through the keyhole shape, the hallway is visible — black steel staircase, herringbone floors
- The keyhole vignette expands as the camera moves forward
- A Homi neon green line traces the keyhole outline as it expands — brand built into the reveal
- **Purpose:** Curiosity. "What's inside?"

### 2.0-3.5s — THE REVEAL
- Keyhole opens fully. We're inside.
- Wide cream living room fills the frame — sectional, olive tree, herringbone floor
- The contrast with the dark keyhole is the emotional hit
- Text drops in Homi green: **"Gewoon huis. Van binnen niet."** (Ordinary house. Not on the inside.)
- **Purpose:** Payoff. The interior is the reward.

### 3.5-5.0s — THE DETAIL
- Quick push into the gold tap on Calacatta marble
- The hero luxury detail shot — holds for a beat
- Homi logo + location lower third: **Helmusweg 12, Venlo**
- **Purpose:** Aspiration + CTA. "I want this."

## Hook Stacking
1. **Visual:** Dark keyhole → golden interior reveal → luxury detail
2. **Text:** "Gewoon huis. Van binnen niet." at the reveal moment
3. **Audio:** Low ambient hum → whoosh as keyhole expands → beat drop on the reveal
4. **Brand:** Green keyhole outline, green text, logo bookend

## Technical Approach
- **Keyhole frame:** Generate with AI (Higgsfield Soul) — dark door, brass keyhole, warm glow
- **Keyhole → Interior:** Kling v3 Pro with start+end frame (keyhole image → interior photo)
- **Interior → Detail:** Kling v3 Pro with start+end frame (living room → gold tap)
- **Branding overlay:** Remotion — green keyhole outline animation, text, logo, audio
- **Format:** 9:16, 1080x1920, TikTok-optimized
