High-volume, high-complexity e-commerce image editing. The foundation of the company.

- Background removal / knockout
- Invisible mannequin / ghost mannequin
- Product retouching (scratches, damages, creases, wrinkles, symmetry, positioning)
- Shadow and reflection creation (natural, drop, reflections)
- Recoloring (every color variant without reshooting)
- Color matching and correction (cross-catalog consistency)
- Masking (hair, fur, fabrics — fuzzy edges)
- (On) model imagery cleanup
- Reshaping and crease removal

**Metrics:** 12M+ images/year | 40K+ images/day | 99.7% on-time | 99.4% first-time-right

**Platform:** STREAM — drag-and-drop, FTP, Auto Fetch/Push, REST API. Built-in Review tool.

**Pricing:** Free self-serve (<2,500 img/yr) | Custom managed (2,500+)

## 2. POSED — AI-Powered On-Model Imagery

Turn standard packshots into on-model imagery using Virtual Models + AI.

**How it works:** Product shot + Virtual Model (from library or custom-created for brand) → AI merges → on-model image in 24 hours.

**Categories:** All wearables — fashion, footwear, bags, jewelry, watches, accessories.

**Metrics:** 900K+ posed images | +14% product engagement vs packshots | 22% of shoppers can't judge size without on-model (Baymard) | 69% leave pages lacking visual info (Salsify)

**Benefits:** Double-digit conversion lift | Shorter studio-to-web time | Fraction of live model shoot cost | Lower returns (better size/fit understanding)

**Unique:** Custom Virtual Model creation exclusive to your brand.
