## 1. Who They Are

Bright River is a Netherlands-based visual content production company with a secondary US presence. They operate at the intersection of AI automation and human creative expertise, specialising in post-production imagery at industrial scale.

- **Headquarters:** Netherlands (phone: +31 (0) 23 542 15 30)
- **US office:** United States (phone: +1 347-645-9535)
- **Founding / size:** Not publicly disclosed on site; they are a mid-to-large specialist studio
- **Platform:** Proprietary SaaS post-production platform called **STREAM**
- **Tagline on logo:** "Visual Product Content Solutions"

They are not a marketplace or freelancer platform — they are an end-to-end managed production partner with their own tooling, AI infrastructure, Client Success Managers, and SLA guarantees.

---

## 2. What They Do

Bright River organises their offer into four product lines:

### POSED — AI-Powered On-Model Imagery
Turn flat product shots into high-quality on-model imagery without a live model shoot. Covers all product categories.

### RENDERED.AR — Interactive 3D & AR
Computer-generated 3D render spins, 2D images, and Augmented Reality experiences. Optimised for major webshops and e-commerce platforms. 40K+ 3D models created to date.

### EDITING — AI-Driven Post-Production
High-volume, high-quality AI-assisted image retouching and editing for e-commerce. Core services include:
- Background removal
- Invisible mannequin / ghost mannequin
- Product retouching (scratches, creases, wrinkles, symmetry)
- Shadow and reflection creation
- Recolouring (eliminate the need to reshoot every colour variant)
- Colour matching and correction
- Masking (hair, fur, fabrics)
- (On) model imagery cleanup

Delivered through STREAM — their online post-production platform. Supports drag-and-drop upload, FTP, Auto Fetch/Push, and REST API integration.

### REAL ESTATE — Visual Content
A dedicated solution vertical for real estate photography businesses (detailed in section 4 below).

They position against:

### Real Estate (most relevant)
- **Real estate photography companies** — studios that shoot properties at volume
- **Real estate agencies / brokerages** with in-house photography operations
- Buyers: Heads of Production, Heads of Customer Success, Heads of Photography

The real estate offering is clearly a growth vertical — they have a named team, a standalone solutions page, and differentiated positioning vs. the e-commerce core.

## 9. Implications for ReelForge Demo UI Copy

When presenting to Bright River's founder, the demo copy should:

1. **Mirror their outcome language** — "deliver faster", "lower cost per listing", "no re-shoots", "consistent quality"
2. **Speak to operational scale** — they think in volumes (listings per day, turnaround hours, rework rates), not one-off projects
3. **Quantify everything** — they are a metrics-first company; vague claims will not land
4. **Lead with real estate** — their RE vertical is strategic; position ReelForge as purpose-built for that workflow
5. **Emphasise the AI-Human hybrid** — they already trust this framing; don't oversell full automation
6. **Remove friction in the demo flow** — their CTAs are always low-commitment; the demo itself should feel "risk-free"
7. **Use their vocabulary**: "workflow", "SLA", "turnaround", "listing", "seamless", "standardized", "cost per listing"
8. **Avoid** marketing-speak they don't use: "revolutionary", "game-changing", "disruptive" — they are pragmatists

Key personas to address in the demo: Head of Production (operational efficiency), Head of Photography (fewer re-shoots, faster editing), and implicitly the CEO/founder (margins and revenue growth).
